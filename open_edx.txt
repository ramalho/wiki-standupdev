====== Open edX ======

> Open edX is an Open Source Web-based system for creating, delivering and an analysing online courses at massive scale.
> //Nate Aune (Appsembler)//

Open edX is also:

  * Open Source (AGPL licensed)
  * A Python application using the Django framework

Open edX numbers (as of 2017):

  * > 800 independent sites running Open exX
  * > 5000 courses
  * > 11 million learners at [[https://www.edx.org/|edX.org]]
  * > 11 million learners at other Open edX sites


===== Infrastructure =====

Minimum of two servers: one for apps, the other for databases (MySQL and MongoDB).

Cost estimate to run an Open edX site on 6 EC2 AWS instances: from USD 950 to USD 1400 per month ([[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=61cahGRFnBQ#t=21m11s|video snippet]]).
